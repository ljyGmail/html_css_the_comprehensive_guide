You have chosen green

The color green is a color of nature of forests and meadows and of spring.

When you are in a good mood, you have a calming effect on other people. You convey security, harmony and hope. Use this mood to start something new in life.

You should be careful, however, if you are in a bad mood, as you can easily become envious of other people. If you seem indifferent to everything and nothing is going on in you life, you are probably overtired and should think about taking a longer break.
